Peel Dosakailu skin take out the seeds and cut them into small pieces and put them in a bowl.
Add turmeric powder,Asafoetida powder, and salt.
In a seperate pan add oil and fry red chilles,fenugreek,urad dal and mustrad seeds until nice aroma comes then grind the popu along with green chillies.
Add popu paste in Dosakai mukkalu,and add very finely chopped Coriander(Cilantro) leaves and mix well.

Goes well with Hot Rice and Ghee.
