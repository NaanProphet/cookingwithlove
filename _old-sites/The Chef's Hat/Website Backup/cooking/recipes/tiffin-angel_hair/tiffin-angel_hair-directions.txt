In a large pot put 6 cups of water and let it boil.In the meantime crush angel hair pasta with hand moderately.
When water starts to boil add 1 tabelspoon oil and add crushed pasta and cook for 2 to 3 mins on a medium high flame.
Then drain pasta into a seive and run cold water through seive for about 5 seconds and keep it a side.
In a pan add oil when oil is hot add red chilly, mustard, and jeera when mustard seeds start to splatter add asafoetida and finely chopped onion(cut into half and cut length wise) and curry leaves.
After onions are nicely fried on medium low heat add cooked pasta and salt and mix well. Its ready to eat.


Goes well with salad or soup.
