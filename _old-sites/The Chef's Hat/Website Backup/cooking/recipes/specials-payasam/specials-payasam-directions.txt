In a big pot add Ghee and fry Charoli and Golden Raisins on medium low heat until raisins puff up.Then take them into separate bowl immediately.
In the remaning Ghee add roasted veermicilli and fry on medium low heat for 1 minute keep stirring so that vermicilli won't burn.
Then add milk, sugar and saffron and cook for 10 mints or until the milk boils on medium low heat without putting lid on stirring in between once.
When milk starts to boil add fried Charoli and Golden Raisins and Elachi powder mix well and off the stove and its ready to eat.

It is a good dessert without much work.
