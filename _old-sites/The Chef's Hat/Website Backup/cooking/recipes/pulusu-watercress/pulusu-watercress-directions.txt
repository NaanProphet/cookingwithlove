Soak mustard seeds, rice, sesame seeds and red chillies in 1/2 cup of water for half an hour and grind them to a paste.
Cut watercress finely.
In a pan put water cress, tamcon tamirnd, turmeric and 2 glasses of water, cover the lid and cook on medium heat for 25 mins.
Then simmer the flame and add rice, sesame, mustard and chilly paste and mix well.
Add salt to taste and cook for another 5 mins on low flame.
For seasoning in a small seperate pan add oil, when oil is hot add mustard seeds, when seeds starting to splatter add aseofotida and off the stove and add seasoning to pulusu and off the stove. 

Watercress pulusu goes well with hot rice and ghee.
