First soak peserapappu in water for two hours.
Peel skin of anapkai and cut in pieces.
Then add start the popu: aavalu, red chilis, jeelakarra
Add cut anapkai on top
Add pesarpappu on top of anapkai (should not be directly touching the bottom -- or else it will burn!)
Add water to let cook and close lid.
Lastly, add salt at end.

Enjoy!
