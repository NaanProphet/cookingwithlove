Peel potatoes and cut into One inch Pieces.
Cut tomatoes in to one inch pieces.
Cut spring onions into 1/2 inch pieces.
In a pan heat oil and add mustard and Jeera seeds when mustard seeds start to splatter add spring onions and fry on medium heat for 2 mints without lid, then add peas tomatoes and potatoes cover the lid and cook on medium low heat for about 20mins or until potatoes are cooked.
Then add turmeric powder, chilly powder, and salt to taste and mix well. Koora is ready to eat.

Goes well with Roti.
