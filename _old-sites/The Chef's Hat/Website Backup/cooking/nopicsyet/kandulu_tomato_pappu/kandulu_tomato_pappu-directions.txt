Soak kandulu overnight.
First, cook kandulu in pressure cooker and add twice amount the water. Add 1/2 tsp (2 pinches) of baking soda as well.
Then start the popu: aavalu (2 tsp), jeelakarra (1 tsp), ingua.
While the popu is cooking, add 10 garlic pearls and then 2 cut tomatoes.
Add tamarind pulp and karvepak. Let cook for a good amount of time, until the tomatoes are completely cooked (and no longer whole).
Once cooked, add 2 spoons sambar powder, 1 spoon chili powder.
Finally add the cooked kandulu, pasupu, and uppu to taste.
