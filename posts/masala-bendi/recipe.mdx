<StyledParagraph>With homemade masala</StyledParagraph>

To prevent bendi from being sticky, first wash and let completely dry for a few hours.

| prep | cook | servings | level |
| :-: | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| 120min | 15min | 2 people | Medium |


- <ListItem>1 pound okra</ListItem>
- <ListItem>3 tbsp red chili powder</ListItem>
- <ListItem>4 tbspn besan flour</ListItem>
- <ListItem>4 tsp cumin powder</ListItem>
- <ListItem>4 tsp coriander powder</ListItem>
- <ListItem>3 tsp dry mango powder</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 1/2 tsp fennel seeds powder</ListItem>
- <ListItem>salt to taste</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 tsp garam masala powder</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 tsp turmeric</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1/2 cup oil</ListItem>

## Preparation

1. Dry roast chana besan first.

2. Then add all the other ingredients.
