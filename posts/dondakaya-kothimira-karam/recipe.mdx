<StyledParagraph>All time classic. If you have patience, freeze them the day before you plan to make them, this makes them crispy on the outside and chewy on the inside.</StyledParagraph>

For onkai kotthira karam, use the same recipe.

| prep | cook | servings | level |
| :-: | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| 10min | 15min | 2 people | Easy |


## Ingredients
- <ListItem>1 pound Dondakayalu</ListItem>
- <ListItem>3 green chillies</ListItem>
- <ListItem>1 big bunch Kothimira (cilantro)</ListItem>
- <ListItem>2 tablespoons sesame oil</ListItem>
- <ListItem>salt to taste</ListItem>

## Preparation

1. Cut dondakayalu lengthwise and cut into half.

2. Grind coarsely green chillies and kothimira (cilantro) in food processor.

3. In a pan put oil then dondakaya pieces. Saute for 1 minute then reduce flame to medium.

4. Cover with lid and cook for another 5 minutes.

5. Remove lid then mix well and cook for another 3 minutes.

6. When the dondakaya pieces are cooked add coarsely ground green chilies, kothimira, and salt.

7. Saute for another 2 minutes on medium heat without lid.

8. Serve with hot rice and ghee.
