## Preparation

1. In 1 tbsp oil, fry peanuts, sesame seeds, coconut, and jeera separately - one by one. Then mix them and grind to paste.

2. To the paste add lengthwise cut green chilies, curry leaves, salt, turmeric powder, chopped kothimira leaves, and chili powder. Mix well.

3. Then deep fry brinjal small with stem slit four sides. Keep them aside.

4. In oil add sajeera, curry leaves, garlic-ginger paste, above grinded masala paste. Mix and cook well until oil separates for about 8 minutes.

5. Then add tamaraind water. Cook well then add fried eggplants, and mix well.

6. Cook for another few minutes.

7. You can also add kaju & siram to the roast and roast everything in little oil.
